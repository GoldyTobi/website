    Pt(554) - 0.1 M HClO4

    echemdb identifier: gomez-marin_2012_surface_558_f1_red

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Pt(554) recorded in 0.1 M HClO4 at a scan rate of 50.0 \(\mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1 in A. M. Gómez-Marín et al. Pt(111) surface disorder kinetics in perchloric acid solutions and the influence of specific anion adsorption. Electrochimica acta, 82:558–569, 2012..

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Pt(554) electrode was prepared by:
    Prior to each experiment the electrodes were flame annealed, cooled in a hydrogen/argon atmosphere and transferred to the cell protected by a drop of ultra-pure water saturated with these gases.

    Comment left by the curator on the published figure
    Focus on Pt oxidation, surface defects (at steps), and sulfate vs. perchlorate adsorption.
