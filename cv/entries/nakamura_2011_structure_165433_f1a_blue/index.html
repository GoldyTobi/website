    Ag(100) - 0.1 M LiBr + 0.05 M LiOH

    echemdb identifier: nakamura_2011_structure_165433_f1a_blue

    A cyclic voltammogramm for Ag(100) recorded in 0.1 M LiBr + 0.05 M LiOH at a scan rate of 50.0 \(\mathrm{mV\,s^{-1}}\) from Figure 1a in M. Nakamura et al. Structure of the electrical double layer on Ag(100): Promotive effect of cationic species on Br adlayer formation. Physical Review B, 84(16):165433, 2011..

    Further information

    The figure shows digitized data.

    The Ag(100) electrode was prepared by:
    Ag electrode etched with chromic acid and then annealed with H2/Ar in induction heating furnace.
